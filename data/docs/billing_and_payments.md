# Billing and Payments Guide

## Subscription Plans and Pricing

| Plan       | Monthly | Annual (save 20%) | API Calls/month | Team Members |
|------------|---------|-------------------|-----------------|--------------|
| Free       | $0      | $0                | 1,000           | 2            |
| Starter    | $29     | $23/mo            | 50,000          | 10           |
| Pro        | $99     | $79/mo            | 500,000         | Unlimited    |
| Enterprise | Custom  | Custom            | Custom          | Unlimited    |

## Accepted Payment Methods
- Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover
- PayPal
- Bank wire transfer (Enterprise plans only)
- Purchase orders (Enterprise plans only)

## Billing Cycle
- Monthly plans: Billed on the same date each month as the initial purchase
- Annual plans: Billed once per year on the anniversary of signup
- Pro-rated charges apply when upgrading mid-cycle
- Downgrades take effect at the end of the current billing period

## Invoices and Receipts
- Invoices are emailed automatically on each billing date
- Download past invoices from Billing > Invoice History
- Contact billing@nexaflow.io for custom invoice formatting or purchase order processing

## Updating Payment Information
1. Go to Settings > Billing > Payment Methods
2. Click "Edit" next to the existing payment method
3. Enter updated card details
4. Click "Save" — your next charge will use the new method

## Failed Payments
If a payment fails:
- NexaFlow will retry the charge after 3 days, then again after 7 days
- You will receive an email notification with a link to update payment information
- After 3 failed attempts, the account enters a grace period of 7 days
- If not resolved, the account is suspended (data is retained for 30 days)

## Upgrading Your Plan
Upgrading takes effect immediately. You will be charged a pro-rated amount for the remainder of the current billing cycle.

## Downgrading Your Plan
Downgrading takes effect at the end of the current billing period. Ensure your usage is within the lower plan's limits before the cycle ends to avoid service interruption.

## Taxes
- US customers: Applicable sales tax added based on your billing address
- EU customers: VAT applied based on country of residence
- Enter your VAT number in Billing > Tax Information to receive VAT-exempt invoices
