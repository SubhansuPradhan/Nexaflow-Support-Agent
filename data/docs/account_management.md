# Account Management Guide

## Updating Account Information

### Changing Your Email Address
1. Go to Settings > Profile > Email
2. Enter your new email address
3. Click "Send Verification"
4. Check both old and new email inboxes — both require verification
5. Click the verification links in both emails within 24 hours

Note: You cannot change your email to one already used by another account.

### Changing Your Display Name
Go to Settings > Profile and update your name. Changes are reflected immediately across all projects.

### Changing Your Password
See the Password Reset Guide.

## Managing Your Organization

### Renaming Your Organization
1. Go to Settings > Organization > General
2. Update the organization name
3. Click "Save Changes"

Note: Changing the organization name does not affect your subdomain URL (e.g., yourcompany.nexaflow.io). To change the subdomain, contact support.

## Account Deletion

### Deleting Your Personal Account
1. Go to Settings > Account > Danger Zone
2. Click "Delete Account"
3. Enter your password to confirm
4. All personal data is permanently deleted within 30 days

You cannot delete your account if you are the sole owner of an organization with active members. Transfer ownership or remove all members first.

### Deleting Your Organization
1. Go to Settings > Organization > Danger Zone
2. Click "Delete Organization"
3. Enter the organization name to confirm
4. All projects, data, and team members are removed within 30 days

## Transferring Ownership
To transfer your organization to another admin:
1. Ensure the recipient has Admin role in your organization
2. Go to Settings > Organization > Ownership
3. Select the new owner from the dropdown
4. Click "Transfer Ownership"
5. The transfer is immediate; your role changes to Member

## Account Suspension
If your account is suspended due to billing issues:
- Data is retained for 30 days
- API access is disabled immediately
- Team members cannot access the account
- Resolve the billing issue in Settings > Billing to reinstate the account

If you believe your account was suspended in error, contact billing@nexaflow.io with your account email and reference number.

## Single Sign-On (SSO)
Enterprise plan customers can configure SSO:
- Supported providers: Google Workspace, Okta, Azure AD, OneLogin
- Configure at Settings > Security > SSO
- Once SSO is enforced, password login is disabled for all team members
- Contact support@nexaflow.io for setup assistance
