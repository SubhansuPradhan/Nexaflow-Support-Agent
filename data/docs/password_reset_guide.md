# Password Reset and Account Access Guide

## Resetting Your Password

### Standard Password Reset
1. Go to https://app.nexaflow.io/login
2. Click "Forgot your password?"
3. Enter your account email address
4. Check your inbox for the reset email (may take up to 5 minutes)
5. Click the link in the email — it expires in 1 hour
6. Enter a new password (minimum 12 characters, must include uppercase, lowercase, number, and special character)
7. Click "Reset Password" — you will be logged in automatically

### If You Did Not Receive the Reset Email
- Check your spam, junk, and promotions folders
- Ensure you are checking the correct email address used to register
- Wait 5 minutes, then request a new reset email
- Add noreply@nexaflow.io to your contacts/safe sender list
- Contact support@nexaflow.io if the issue persists after 10 minutes

## Account Locked After Failed Login Attempts
Accounts are temporarily locked after 5 consecutive failed login attempts to protect against unauthorized access.

### How to Unlock Your Account
- Wait 30 minutes — the account unlocks automatically
- Or use the Password Reset flow above to regain immediate access
- If you believe this was a suspicious login attempt, contact security@nexaflow.io immediately

## Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

### Setting Up 2FA
1. Go to Settings > Security > Two-Factor Authentication
2. Choose your method: Authenticator App (recommended) or SMS
3. For Authenticator App: Scan the QR code with Google Authenticator or Authy
4. Enter the 6-digit code to verify and enable 2FA

### Lost Access to Your 2FA Device
1. Use a backup code (saved during 2FA setup) to log in
2. Once logged in, disable 2FA in Settings > Security and re-enroll
3. If you do not have backup codes, contact support@nexaflow.io with your account email, company name, and a government-issued ID for identity verification

## Changing Your Password While Logged In
1. Go to Settings > Security > Password
2. Enter your current password
3. Enter and confirm your new password
4. Click "Update Password"
5. All active sessions will be logged out except the current one

## Password Requirements
- Minimum 12 characters
- At least one uppercase letter (A-Z)
- At least one lowercase letter (a-z)
- At least one number (0-9)
- At least one special character (!@#$%^&*)
- Cannot reuse the last 5 passwords
