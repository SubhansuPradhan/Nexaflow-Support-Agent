# Frequently Asked Questions

## General

**Q: Is NexaFlow suitable for small teams?**
A: Yes. The Free plan supports up to 2 team members and 3 projects at no cost. The Starter plan at $29/month covers teams of up to 10 with 50,000 API calls per month.

**Q: Can I use NexaFlow without technical knowledge?**
A: Absolutely. The dashboard is designed for non-technical users. API access and webhooks are optional features for developers.

**Q: Does NexaFlow have a mobile app?**
A: Yes. iOS and Android apps are available on the App Store and Google Play. The mobile apps support task management, notifications, and project viewing. API key management and billing are desktop-only.

**Q: Is there an offline mode?**
A: The mobile apps support viewing recently accessed projects offline. Creating and updating tasks requires an internet connection.

## Billing

**Q: Will I be charged immediately when I upgrade?**
A: Yes. You are charged a pro-rated amount for the remainder of the current billing cycle, then the full new plan price on the next renewal date.

**Q: Can I switch from monthly to annual billing?**
A: Yes. Go to Settings > Billing > Change Billing Cycle. The switch takes effect on your next renewal date. You will be charged the annual rate and receive a 20% discount.

**Q: What happens to my data if I downgrade?**
A: Your data is preserved. However, if your usage exceeds the lower plan's limits (e.g., more than 10 team members on Starter), you must reduce usage before the downgrade takes effect.

**Q: Do you offer nonprofit or educational discounts?**
A: Yes. Nonprofits and accredited educational institutions receive a 30% discount. Email billing@nexaflow.io with proof of status.

## Technical

**Q: What is the maximum file attachment size?**
A: 50MB per file. For larger files, store them in Google Drive or Dropbox and link them to the task.

**Q: Does NexaFlow support custom domains?**
A: Enterprise customers can use a custom domain (e.g., projects.yourcompany.com). Contact support to configure.

**Q: How many API keys can I create?**
A: Free: 2. Starter: 10. Pro: 50. Enterprise: Unlimited.

**Q: Does NexaFlow support IPv6?**
A: Yes, all NexaFlow endpoints are accessible over both IPv4 and IPv6.

## Security

**Q: Is my data backed up?**
A: Yes. Data is backed up every 6 hours to geographically separate storage. Backups are retained for 90 days. Point-in-time recovery is available for Enterprise customers.

**Q: Can I enable SSO for my team?**
A: SSO is available on the Enterprise plan. Supported providers include Google Workspace, Okta, Azure AD, and OneLogin.

**Q: What should I do if I suspect unauthorized access?**
A: Immediately go to Settings > Security > Active Sessions and revoke all sessions. Change your password and enable 2FA. Then email security@nexaflow.io with your account email and a description of the suspicious activity.

## Support

**Q: How do I contact support?**
A: Email support@nexaflow.io. Pro and Enterprise customers can also access live chat from the dashboard (bottom-right Help button). Enterprise customers have a dedicated Slack channel with their Customer Success Manager.

**Q: Is there a community forum?**
A: Yes. Join the NexaFlow community at community.nexaflow.io to ask questions, share tips, and vote on feature requests.
